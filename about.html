This Comic

It follows the story of Sophia, a woman in a high position at a relatively young age, coming back to her first alma mater after a long time away from her hometown. A ghost from the past resurfaces that starts hitting on her, and from there spirals down into a very intimate and personal journey of facing her true self.

The story in general is about older women facing troubles, just when they thought they figured it all out.

This comic updates monthly with a whole chapter.

This Author

Sarejusta is an artist with storytelling and world-building close to her heart. With a multidisciplinary approach, she is professionally active through freelance in visdev, concept and comics-oriented work. In her free time, she enjoys cooking, gaming, TTRPGs (you’ll take Pathfinder from her cold, dead hands) and painting Warhammer figurines.
